Lift Logic

Lift Logic is an innovative app designed specifically for powerlifters to optimize their training and maximize performance. By utilizing the Rate of Perceived Exertion (RPE) scale, the app helps users gauge their effort during workouts, allowing them to adjust their training intensity according to their physical and mental state. With features that include customizable workout logs, progress tracking, and expert coaching insights, Lift Logic empowers users to train smarter and more effectively. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, Lift Logic is your essential companion for achieving your powerlifting goals.
